I am a third-year Ph.D. Student in the Autonomous Systems Laboratory in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Cornell University. My advisor is Prof. Hadas Kress-Gazit. I am interested in the synthesis of provably-correct controllers for complex robotic systems. My work draws from a broad spectrum of ideas, including optimization, hybrid systems, formal methods, and control systems.

Prior to Cornell and after receiving my B.S. and M.S. degrees at Virginia Tech, I spent several years in industry as an aerospace engineer at NASA and Impact Tech (now Sikorsky). While at NASA, I participated in the development of the software tool C-MAPSS.
