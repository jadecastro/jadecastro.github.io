Jonathan A. DeCastro

 

I am a third-year Ph.D. Student in the Autonomous Systems Laboratory in the Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Cornell University. My advisor is Prof. Hadas Kress-Gazit. I am interested in the synthesis of provably-correct controllers for complex robotic systems. My work leverages a broad spectrum of ideas, including optimization, formal methods, and control systems.
